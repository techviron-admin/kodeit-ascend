Frequently asked questions

The answers teachers and leaders look for first

Go to Assessments and choose New assessment. Pick a framework and standard, and let the AI draft the items. Review the questions, adjust the DOK mix, and publish to your classes. Most teachers ship their first assessment in under ten minutes.
The engine parses the full text of every standard in your selected framework and tags each generated item with the exact code, DOK level, and competency. Every item stays in a review queue until a human approves it — nothing reaches students unreviewed.
Yes. Upload CSV, QTI, or Word documents from Question Bank → Import. The engine auto-tags imported items to your framework and flags anything it cannot align confidently, so you only review the edge cases.
CCSS Mathematics and ELA, NGSS, IB MYP, PISA, and US state frameworks ship out of the box. Custom frameworks can be added from Competencies → Add framework and mapped to the unified taxonomy.
Kodeit Ascend is FERPA and COPPA aligned with SOC 2 Type II controls. Data is encrypted in transit and at rest, access is scoped by role and school, and districts retain full ownership of their records.
Administrators can invite staff from User Management → Invite user. Teachers receive an email link and are scoped to their school and classes automatically. Bulk invites via CSV are supported for larger rollouts.
